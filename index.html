    Expermental Prints

    In my artistic practice, I explore the intricate and profound connections between the human spirit and the natural world through experimental printmaking. My works serve as a bridge between the tangible textures of trees and other elements of nature with the intangible essence of human existence. Utilizing techniques such as monoprint, silkscreen, and collagraphy, I strive to create pieces that evoke a sense of unity and harmony, inviting viewers to reflect on our intertwined destinies with the earth’s natural beauty.

    SilkScreen Prints

    Silkscreen printmaking is a vibrant dance of color and form, a medium where each layer tells a story and every squeegee pull is a deliberate stroke of intention. My silkscreen art is a visual symphony, harmonizing the boldness of graphic imagery with the subtleties of human and natural forms. It’s a celebration of texture, pattern, and the tactile nature of printmaking, inviting viewers to explore the intricate connections between the ink, the screen, and the shared narratives of our environment.

  • Digital Works

    My digital creations stand alone as contemporary art and serve as the foundation for my silkscreen prints. They are a fusion of printmaking tradition and digital innovation, embodying the essence of both worlds in a singular artistic expression.
